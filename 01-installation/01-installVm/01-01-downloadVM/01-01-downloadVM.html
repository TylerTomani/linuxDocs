Downloading a Virtual Machine

Visit virtualbox.org/downloads, as of 2022 the page looks like this,

 Click on the platform package based on your operating system.
The neccessary requirements on your host hardrive are:

  • Enough RAM for the Virtual Machine and Host
  • At least 10GBs of memory for each VM "Virtual Machine"
    on the host hardrive
  • Supported host operating system. Currently VMs
    are supported on:
    • Windows
    • Linux
    • MacOS 10
    • Solaris Unix

 Open the downloaded dmg and follow the instructions.
Choose the default settings.
